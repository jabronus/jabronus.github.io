The Four Evangelists

I love digital illustration. I love it a whole lot, but haven’t been able to do as much of it as I’d like over the last year at Southland. So when my boss told me about Jon’s plan to teach through the New Testament in 2015 starting with the Gospels, I jumped at the chance to kick it off with some ‘ole Christian iconography.

Lions and Oxen and Eagles Oh My

I first became aware of the concept of the Four Evangelists while checking out Adam Lewis Greene’s Bibliotheca Kickstarter. As one of the tier support rewards, he offered a letterpressed print of the Four Evangelists with a central Christogram.

Growing up in Wesleyan/Methodist churches, I was never much exposed to ancient Christian iconography. The closest I got might have been some of the stuff I saw in art and humanities classes, or metaphors referred to in hymns or classical art music.
